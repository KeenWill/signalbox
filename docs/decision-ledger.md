# Decision ledger

This is the authoritative inventory of accepted and unresolved foundational questions. A “leaning” guides exploration but is not a decision. Closing a row requires an accepted ADR or an explicit later-scope decision. Scenario identifiers refer to [scenarios.md](scenarios.md).

Unless a row says otherwise, **owner/reviewer status** is “repository owner decision pending; specialist reviewer unassigned.” This status applies individually to every row below.

Rows marked **Foundation accepted** inherit this status: the repository owner accepted ADR-0001, ADR-0003, ADR-0004, ADR-0005, and ADR-0027 atomically on 2026-07-13 after independent adversarial architecture review; no specialist human reviewer was assigned.

## Ledger maintenance

An ADR must update or close every ledger row it decides, link the affected scenarios and invariants, and list explicit non-decisions. A prototype may inform a leaning without changing a row to decided.
